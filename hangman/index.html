ABOUT HANGMAN

Hangman is a word-guessing game where players guess a hidden word within a set number of tries. Wrong guesses result in parts of a Hangman figure being drawn. It offers various categories like animals, countries, and sports, with different difficulty levels. Players aim to guess the word before the Hangman figure is complete. With options for beginners to experts, Hangman provides a challenge for all skill levels.

  • Ready to play?

How to Play

  • Choose a category and difficulty level
  • Guess the word with each dash representing a letter in the word.
  • If you make a correct guess, the letter will be revealed
  • If you guess incorrectly, a part of the Hangman figure will be drawn.
  • Your goal is to guess the word correctly before the Hangman figure is fully drawn

    • Remember to have fun!!!
